description: "Start or continue a brainstorming session with real-time capture"
---

Read the skill definition at `.forge/skills/brainstorm-capture/SKILL.md` and follow its instructions.

First, read `.forge/memory/brainstorm-capture.md` to check for any active brainstorm session.

Then enter BRAINSTORM MODE:
- Engage conversationally with Logan about the topic
- Ask clarifying questions, propose alternatives, challenge assumptions
- CONTINUOUSLY update `.forge/memory/brainstorm-capture.md` with structured notes
- Log every decision with reasoning, every rejected alternative with why
- Maintain a running Decisions Summary at the bottom

When Logan says "plan this", "plan it", "compile", or similar:
- Stop brainstorming
- Read the brainstorm-capture.md you've been maintaining
- Synthesize selected decisions into a plan at `.forge/plans/active-plan.md`
- Then suggest running `/forge-compile` to generate drift-prevention artifacts
